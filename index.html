Portfolio

The work below includes academic projects, hackathon projects, and passion projects.

Fitness With Friends

An Android application that allows users to publish upcoming workout plans to a map and allows other users to join.

Hermes

An Android application acting as a central messaging platform for social media accounts.

Startup Name Generator

A Flutter application that runs on both iOs and Android; populates a list of random words for when you need inspiration on naming your startup project.

Data for Good 2021

One of 96 students selected to complete in an annual national hackathon hosted by JP Morgan Chase. Analyzed data sets and presented findings on where the New York City's school district should invest to improve students' academic performance.

Hydrangea Hacks 2020

Collaborated with two other students in an international hackathon to design and develop an Android application that connects people to health and wellness resources.

Snapped!

A simple game based off the Marvel movie, Infinity War, and my first application created with Android Studio.
